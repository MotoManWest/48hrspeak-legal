Privacy Policy for 48Hr Speak

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to 48Hr Speak (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application 48Hr Speak (the “App”).

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Name (if you sign in with Apple Sign In)
  • Apple User ID (if you sign in with Apple Sign In)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

2. Learning Data

To provide personalized language learning, we collect:

  • Language selection (which language you’re learning)
  • Learning progress (completed lessons, modules, scores)
  • Study history (time spent, lessons accessed)
  • AI Tutor conversations (messages exchanged with the AI tutor)

3. Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, we collect:

  • Subscription status (active, canceled, expired)
  • Subscription tier (monthly, yearly, lifetime)
  • Purchase history (managed through Apple App Store)
  • Payment information (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

4. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (features used, session duration, app performance)
  • Error logs (crash reports, technical diagnostics)

5. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Credit card numbers (handled by Apple)
  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or camera access (unless explicitly granted for specific features)
  • Microphone data (unless explicitly granted for speech practice features)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. Provide and Improve Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver personalized language learning content
  • Power the AI Tutor with conversation history
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve app features and user experience

2. Process Subscriptions

  • Manage your subscription status
  • Provide access to premium features (AI Tutor)
  • Send subscription renewal reminders
  • Process refunds when applicable

3. Communicate With You

  • Send important account updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send learning reminders (if enabled)
  • Notify you of new features or updates

4. Ensure Security

  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect your information:

1. Supabase (Backend & Database)

  • Purpose: User authentication, data storage
  • Data Shared: Email, user ID, learning progress
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Location: Data stored in secure cloud servers

2. RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Manage in-app subscriptions
  • Data Shared: User ID, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Location: United States

3. OpenAI (AI Tutor)

  • Purpose: Power the AI language tutor
  • Data Shared: Conversation messages, language context
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Location: United States
  • Note: Conversations are processed but not used to train AI models

4. Apple App Store (Payments)

  • Purpose: Process subscription payments
  • Data Shared: Purchase information
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Note: We never see your payment information

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • AI Conversations: Stored for up to 90 days for service improvement, then anonymized or deleted
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Your Privacy Rights

All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

  2. Go to the home screen
  3. Tap “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm deletion

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and token-based authentication
  • Access Control: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments
  • Secure Storage: Cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@48hrspeak.com, and we will delete such information.
